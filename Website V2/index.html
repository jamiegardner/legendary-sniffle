Canon C100 Professional Video Camera

Canon Cameras

The Canon C100 is a versatile video camera offering the user many different options and settings to get the right shot for their project. This camera while expensive at a retail price of around Â£2000, not including any lenses you may want to purchase on top of that, is perfect for any professional film or television projects.

It includes an 8.3MP Super 35mm CMOS sensor which Canon has design and manufactured specifically for video capture. Canon claims it delivers superb high-resolution images with high sensitivity, low

low noise, reduced rolling shutter artefacts and beautifully shallow depth of field. The C100 offers a variety of outputs for video including recording to SD, SDHC and SDXC cards with relay and simultaneous recording options. It also includes HDMI, AV and USB output ports.

For audio, the C100 has two XLR inputs and a stereo microphone, perfect for connecting a mounted microphone or boom mic. The C100 has a great battery life - only requires one battery to power it.

Pros

Versatile

The C100 video camera offers the user many different options and settings to get the right shot for their project.

Auto-Focusing

The C100 enables the user to use the automatic auto-focus function which allows the user to prioritise other things.

Battery Life

This video camera provides the user with great battery life for filming, so doesn't need to be recharged as much.

Cons

Price

The C100 is quite expensive for the user but it's worth it for the filming quality.

No 50p, 60p or high-speed-shooting

The C100 does not offer any high speed shooting, so if you're after a camera that does this, you might want to skip this one.

Shoulder mount requires extra purchase

If you are the type of filmmaker that needs to use their camera on a shoulder mount, you'll need to purchase that attachment separately.

Sony NX3 Video Camera

Sony Cameras

The Sony NX3 video camera is a top of the line professional video camera capable of high quality video output. The NX3 retails for nearly Â£2000 and its easy to see why. The camera comes with a variety of features any beginner or professional filmmaker would require.

The NX3 offers a variety of features any filmaker will want to produce high quality video content. The 1920x1080 full HD 3x Exmor CMOS sensors system provides a 2.07 million effective pixel count in

16:9 aspect ratio, which provides very high quality video output. The NX3 shoots to standard SD cards and has a and HDMI output. Its audio output uses two XLR inputs for microphones and comes with a mic clamp on the front and a stereo 3.5mm jack for headphones.

If you need a versatile camera that functions well and offers the user a quick set up to achieve high quality shots, this is the camera for you. It can easily be operated by a single person - great for solo projects.

Pros

Easy to use and operate by one person

The sony NX3 video camera doesn't require many people to use and operate for filming projects.

Features auto-focus, auto white balance and auto iris available

These three features will defiantly improve the quality of the videos for your project.

Variety of output ports

This video camera has lots of different output ports for many uses of your filming projects.

Cons

Price

The price for this video camera is expensive - however the quality and ease of usability is worth it.

Not ideal for shoulder mounted shooting

This video camera is not suitable for any other filming attachments for you filming projects.

No detachable lens

The camera doesn't offer the user the capability of having detachable lenses so this may make filming difficult.
